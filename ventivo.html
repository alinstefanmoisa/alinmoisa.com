About

Ventivo is a fast-growing software development & outsourcing business focused on quality, people and nature.

Scope

Ventivo wished to position themselves as a fresh corporate company on the market.

From my research they were loosing in the favor of other companies that looked more modern and had an online presence. Design an online presence were key metrics for them that directly impacted the net revenue of the company.

My objective was to create the visual design for them. Developed brand identity, visual deliverables and to create a visually striking design for an essential reach.

What I've done

Working on this project my role was to develop the marketing materials, to develop the visual design, to identify users needs, to develop wireframes, and to develop a user-friendly interface.

Additionally,I worked in the creation and development of the product’s visual styleguide. Worked closely with the development team to ensure optimal implementation.

Metrics

The key to successfully designing a marketing website and help them have an online presence is to keep the stakeholders need in mind and look at the competitors presence.

The next step was to analyze the competiton design of the online presence. We identified the strengths and weaknesses of the companies and we determine which aspects need to be taken in consideration.

This helped us to create a design that builds on what already works well.

Once we've created the design, we've used an interactive prototype to test it with users to ensure that it meets their needs and expectations. Based on team feedback, we iterated the design and made improvements as needed.

Iconography

It wasn’t difficult to reach the conclusion that Ventivo need to have an extensive icon set, along with a robust set of rules and constrains

The visual metaphor that unifies the Ventivo iconic ecosystem is the point. More specifically, it is the (point) shape used in the Ventivo logo. It is usually placed in the lower right corner to act as a visual sign-off.

Also the shape - its form, can be used to build icons for social media. Another possible use is that of a photo container. The iconic ecosystem is dynamic, it will evolve according to the specific needs of each communication situation.

Supporting
Visuals

Supporting visuals refers to any graphical or visual elements, such as images, charts, graphs, diagrams, photographs, illustrations, or multimedia content, that are used in various forms of communication, including presentations, reports, websites, and educational materials.

These visuals are employed to complement and enhance the understanding of the accompanying text or spoken information. Supporting visuals are intended to provide additional context, clarify complex concepts, emphasize key points, or engage the audience in a more compelling and informative manner.

They play a crucial role in aiding comprehension, retention, and overall communication effectiveness, making complex or abstract information more accessible and engaging to the audience.

Results

Elevating the overall quality and user satisfaction of their software products. Contributed and helped establish a strong foundation for their company's success.

Enabling them to deliver exceptional digital experiences that surpassed client expectations.
