About

This project aims to digitalize construction activities. The system is quite complex, having functionalities ranging from planning work to monitoring what workers do and maintaining activities up to date.

What I’ve done

Working on this project my role was to performed in-depth user research to comprehensively evaluate the pain points and distinct requirements, engaged in iterative ideation processes to devise innovative solutions for various challenges, developed user flows, wireframes, high-fidelity prototypes, and conducted systematic design and testing procedures to iterate and refine the design solutions.

Mobile App

Presented here are a series of screens from the app, intentionally avoiding complete flows to preserve the confidentiality of certain app features.

My focus here is to provide a glimpse into the design without revealing all aspects of the app's functionality.

Conclusion

The prototype and the project received positive feedback from our stakeholders and clients.

The solution is not implemented yet, but we took the chance to explore different paths to take, experiment with ideas and gather feedback for the proposed solution.
