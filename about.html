SEE MY WORK

At work

I am deeply committed to developing and maintaining a design that enhances user experience and ensures visual consistency for a wide range of businesses.

My background includes working with print, digital agencies and software companies, from small to large international technology companies, giving me a diverse range of experience and skills.

Over the past years, I have advanced from a Graphic Designer to a UX/UI Designer which has allowed me to expand my understanding of the overall strategic vision. The new role allowed me to learn more about my future career path and growth opportunities.

Besides professional work

When I'm not working on projects, you may find me travelling around Europe with my girlfriend and my two dogs (Sasha & Minnie) or just enjoying some music and a cup of coffee.

My hobbies include things like hiking, carving, going out, health, reading, psychology, technology, learning something new and why not, watching a good movie.

Process

RESEARCH IDEATION

Discover

My approach is made to fit the project needs. I take in account business goals, the target market, and the technical constrains in order to create a product that is practical and appealing.

WIREFRAMING PROTOTYPING

Define

I define the target audience, do the competitive analysis and the research the specific needs and pain points, and then focus on building a product that solves those problems, but take the business needs in close range.

DESIGN VISUAL DESIGN

Develop

In a successful product design timeline, the product will meet the specifications of the business, developing the brand, solve the user's problem and provide aesthetic value.

FINAL DESIGN HAND OFF

Deliver

When I design product, I design for people for who I deliver the right features with the right user experience.
